sasi engineering college

Sasi Institute Of Technology & Engineering is running under Sasi Educational Society. The institute was established in the year 2002 in the Business hub of West Godavari district, Tadepalligudem town and is well connected to both road & rail transport. SITE with its academic discipline, results and the achievement of its students has made its presence more in the whole region. The campus is located in the lush green area in an area of more than 28 acres offering world class facilities. Due to the strong technical and soft skills of the students, the college is the first choice for the companies to come here for recruitment and for the same reason this college is among the favourite place for the parents to send their wards in good academic environment.
